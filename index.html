Brief Summary:

Get Rich or Die Tryin' 50 Cent's debut album took storm when released on Feburary 6th, 2003. Record labels Interscope Records, Eminem's Shady Records, Dr. Dre's Aftermath Entertainment and also 50 Cent's own G-Unit Records all released this album and had a part in this project. Get Rich or Die Tryin' charted at number one on the Billboard 200 while selling over 872,000 copies in only the first week of sales. The singles, particularly, In da Club and 21 Questions also saw much success as they both, at one point, reached number one on the Billboard Hot 100. The album featured other famous artists like Eminem and Nate Dogg, and other artists from 50 Cent's G-Unit group such as LLoyd Banks, and Tony Yayo. Overall the album was a major hit and was the overall best-selling album of the year selling more than 12 million copies by the end of 2003.
